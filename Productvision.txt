The fundamental concept in this project is attendance maintenance, which is used to generate
reports and create leave report forms. Everything has been computerized in our age of everincreasing technologies. The Human workforce has grown as a result of the abundance of job
options. As a result, a system that can handle the data of such a vast number of employees in an
organization is required. Because of its user-friendly design, this project makes maintaining
records easier. The " ATTENDANCE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM" is created to address the
issues that plagued the previous manual system. This software is being developed in order to
